# Authoring guide: TIME_PRESSURE_SURVIVAL

This is the cave-game / heist-game shape: a timed chapter with a deadline, survival resources that deplete with the clock, and an out-of-time ending. Both "The Sump Line" and "The Countinghouse" use this preset.

The full method — branch-and-bottleneck structure, clock calibration, survival-resource tuning, and the ending-honesty rules — is in [`docs/authoring-method.md`](../authoring-method.md).
