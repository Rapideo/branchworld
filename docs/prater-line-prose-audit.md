## 4. EE-4 Verdict

**Pass (after two fixes).** Every load-bearing prose claim in the five endings and six state-gated variant nodes (`node_sperl_trust`, `node_get_real_film`, `node_handoff_witnessed`, `node_canal_drop`, `node_volkov` + `take_deal` condition, `node_volkov_truth`) maps to a flag that is set on the path that reaches it.

**Convention:** An ending may narrate only facts implied by its resolver conditions; branch-specific props (decoy, companion, specific clues) must be hedged or omitted.

**Two violations were found and fixed, both in `ending_burned` ("Smoke on the Embankment").**

**Fix 1 (companion — first pass).** Original prose narrated "You board the 02:10 alone." as fact. This was an EE-4 violation: every forward path that reaches `ending_burned` passes through a Sperl exit choice that sets `companion = 'dragomir'` (e.g. `take_decoy_leave` in `node_sperl_press_film` sets companion while still yielding `dragomir_trust < 3`). The prior audit argument — that trust < 3 prevents `companion` from being set — was wrong. The choices that set `companion = dragomir` do not require elevated trust; they require only leaving Café Sperl toward the crossroads. Fix: prose rewritten to companion-agnostic conditional phrasing — "Whether she is sitting across from you now … or whether the wet streets swallowed her … the failure is the same" — mirroring `ending_missed`.

**Fix 2 (decoy possession — second pass).** The first-pass fix left adjacent prose asserting "The steel canister in your coat is light as a lie — because that is what it is. The decoy." as universal fact. The decoy is only handed over on the `press_film` branch (`node_sperl_press_film`); there is no flag tracking it. A reachable burned path that never visits `node_sperl_press_film` (e.g. `take_satchel → tram → cover → get_moving → to_riesenrad → down_to_canal → wait_for_handoff → find_volkov → refuse_volkov`) carries no canister at all. This is a branch-specific prop narrated as universal — the same class of violation as the "alone" lie. Fix: rewritten to "Whatever you carry now — the decoy she shoved across the marble, or nothing at all — it weighs the same: nothing. You never earned the real film." — possession-agnostic, true on every burned path.

**State-backed claims preserved intact:** no real film (`has_real_microfilm = false`), broken trust (`dragomir_trust < 3`), and Volkov's closed net (`volkov_suspicion >= 4`) are all genuine resolver conditions and remain narrated as fact. No companion or prop claim is asserted without backing.

No further prose claims reference a flag absent on the reaching path.
